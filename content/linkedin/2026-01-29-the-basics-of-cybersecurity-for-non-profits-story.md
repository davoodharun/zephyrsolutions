Last year, a small nonprofit faced a cyber incident that shook its operations. They were working on a fundraising campaign when they discovered unauthorized access to their donor database. Thankfully, they had implemented basic cybersecurity measures, which limited the damage. 

This experience taught them the importance of knowing their data, educating their staff, and using strong passwords. The team came together, revamped their cybersecurity plan, and even hosted a workshop to raise awareness. 

Now, they feel more secure and ready to focus on their mission without fear. Remember, it’s never too late to start your cybersecurity journey. If you’re interested, I’d be happy to share some practical tips to help your organization stay safe.
