Here are some essential cybersecurity basics every nonprofit should consider: 
1. Use strong, unique passwords for all accounts. 
2. Enable two-factor authentication wherever possible. 
3. Educate staff about phishing and other common threats. 
4. Regularly update software and systems to patch vulnerabilities. 
5. Backup important data regularly and securely. 
6. Create a cybersecurity policy to guide your organization. 
7. Monitor your systems for unusual activity.
