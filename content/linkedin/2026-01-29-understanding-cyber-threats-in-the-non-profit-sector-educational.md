Are your organization's digital assets at risk? In today's digital landscape, small nonprofits face unique challenges when it comes to cybersecurity. From phishing attacks to data breaches, understanding these threats is crucial. 

Many nonprofits believe they're too small to be targeted, but the reality is that cybercriminals often seek out vulnerable organizations. A simple email scam can lead to lost donations or compromised donor information. 

It's important to regularly assess your organization's digital practices. Consider implementing basic security measures like strong passwords, regular software updates, and staff training on recognizing suspicious emails.
