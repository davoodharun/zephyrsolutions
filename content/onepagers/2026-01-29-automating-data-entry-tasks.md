# Automating Data Entry Tasks for Nonprofits

## Risk/Impact
- **Time Savings**: Automating data entry can free up valuable time for your team to focus on mission-driven activities.
- **Error Reduction**: Manual data entry often leads to mistakes; automation can help improve accuracy.
- **Resource Allocation**: Redirect resources towards outreach and engagement instead of repetitive tasks.

## What Good Looks Like
- **Streamlined Processes**: Data flows smoothly from collection to storage without bottlenecks.
- **User-Friendly Tools**: Easy-to-use systems that staff can quickly learn without extensive training.
- **Reliable Data**: Consistent, accurate data that enhances reporting and decision-making.
