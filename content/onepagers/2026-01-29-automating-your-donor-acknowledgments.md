# Automating Your Donor Acknowledgments

## Risk/Impact
- **Donor Trust**: Timely acknowledgments help build and maintain trust with your supporters.
- **Efficiency**: Manually sending acknowledgments can be time-consuming and prone to errors.
- **Engagement**: Consistent communication fosters stronger relationships with donors, encouraging future support.

## What Good Looks Like
- Acknowledgments are sent within 48 hours of receiving a donation.
- Messages are personalized, acknowledging the donorâ€™s contribution and impact.
- Automated systems track and manage donor information seamlessly.

## Recommended Next 30 Days
1. **Research Tools**: Identify and review software options that can automate acknowledgment processes.
2. **Create Templates**: Draft personalized acknowledgment templates for different giving levels.
3. **Pilot Program**: Select a small group of donors to test the automated acknowledgment process.
4. **Gather Feedback**: Collect responses from donors and team members to refine the process.

## Cost Bands
- **$**: Basic email automation tools (e.g., Mailchimp, Constant Contact)
- **$$**: Integrated donor management software with acknowledgment features (e.g., DonorPerfect, Bloomerang)
- **$$$**: Custom solutions or high-end donor management systems with full automation capabilities (e.g., Blackbaud, Salesforce for Nonprofits)

---

Consider starting small and scaling as you learn what works best for your organization.
