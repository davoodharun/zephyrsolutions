# Automating Your Donor Management

## Risk/Impact
- **Inefficient Processes**: Manual donor management can lead to errors and lost opportunities.
- **Donor Engagement**: Without automation, keeping donors informed and engaged can be challenging.
- **Data Overload**: Managing large amounts of donor data manually can be overwhelming and prone to mistakes.

## What Good Looks Like
- **Streamlined Communication**: Automated thank-you notes and updates keep donors engaged effortlessly.
- **Organized Data**: All donor information is easily accessible and organized for quick decision-making.
- **Increased Donations**: Efficient management leads to better donor relationships and potentially increased contributions.

## Recommended Next 30 Days
- **Assess Current Tools**: Review existing donor management practices and identify pain points.
- **Research Options**: Explore different donor management systems that fit your organization’s needs.
- **Pilot Program**: Consider running a small pilot with a selected tool to gauge effectiveness before a full rollout.

## Cost Bands
- **$**: Basic tools (e.g., spreadsheets, simple CRM) – Affordable, but may lack advanced features.
- **$$**: Mid-tier software (e.g., specialized donor management systems) – Moderate investment with better functionality.
- **$$$**: Comprehensive solutions (e.g., all-in-one platforms) – Higher cost, but often includes extensive support and features.
