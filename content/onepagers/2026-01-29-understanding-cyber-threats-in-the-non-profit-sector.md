# Understanding Cyber Threats in the Non-Profit Sector

## Risk/Impact
- Cyber threats can disrupt your operations, leading to loss of donor trust and financial resources.
- Sensitive data, like donor information, can be targeted, risking privacy and compliance.

## What Good Looks Like
- A strong cybersecurity posture includes regular updates to software and systems.
- Staff are trained and aware of potential threats, creating a culture of security.
- Data is backed up regularly and stored securely to prevent loss.

## Recommended Next 30 Days
1. **Assess Current Security**: Evaluate your existing systems and identify vulnerabilities.
2. **Staff Training**: Schedule a short training session on recognizing phishing and other common threats.
3. **Update Software**: Ensure all software is up to date with the latest security patches.
